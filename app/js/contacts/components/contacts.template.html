The system for contolling the achievements and activity of the primary school pupils.

The main functional opportunities are as follows:

  • The registration of the school and giving the admin the permission to manage the database of the school, levels, groups, teachers, parents, and students.
  • Writing down the School/Achievements Journal (the journal of activity ) of each student and the class in general.
  • The parents have the opportunity to view the reports on the activity of their children.
  • Making the bidirectional association between the parents and the teachers with the help of the online chat.
